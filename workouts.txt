    Bench Press: 4 sets x 8-12 reps
        This is a compound movement that targets the overall chest.

    Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets x 10-15 reps
        Focuses on the upper chest and helps create a well-rounded chest appearance.

    Chest Flyes: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
        Isolates the chest muscles, emphasizing the stretch and contraction.

    Push-Ups (Weighted if Possible): 3 sets x 12-15 reps
        A bodyweight exercise that targets the chest and can be modified for different intensity levels.

    Decline Bench Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
        Emphasizes the lower part of the chest, completing the development of the entire chest.

Triceps Workouts:

    Tricep Dips: 4 sets x 10-15 reps
        Targets the triceps and also engages the chest and shoulders.

    Tricep Rope Pushdown: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
        Isolation exercise for the triceps, focusing on the pushdown motion.

    Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extension: 3 sets x 12-15 reps
        Works the long head of the triceps and helps with overall arm development.

    Skull Crushers (Barbell or Dumbbell): 3 sets x 10-12 reps
        Targets the triceps and requires controlled movement to avoid strain.

    Close-Grip Bench Press: 3 sets x 10-12 reps
        Places emphasis on the triceps and is an excellent compound movement.
